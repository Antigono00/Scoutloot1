Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how ScoutLoot ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website at scoutloot.com.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

2. Cookies We Use

ScoutLoot uses minimal cookies and local storage to provide essential functionality. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

Name Purpose Duration
scoutloot_user Stores your login session to keep you signed in Until logout or cleared
scoutloot_cookie_consent Remembers that you accepted our cookie notice Permanent

3. Local Storage

In addition to cookies, we use browser local storage to:

  • Store your user session information for faster page loads
  • Remember your cookie consent preference

Local storage data remains on your device until you clear your browser data or log out.

4. Essential vs Non-Essential

All cookies and local storage we use are essential for the service to function. We do not use any non-essential cookies for:

  • Advertising or marketing
  • Third-party tracking
  • Analytics (we don't use Google Analytics or similar services)
  • Social media integration

5. Third-Party Services

Our website loads resources from the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: We load fonts from fonts.googleapis.com. Google may set cookies according to their own privacy policy.

We do not share any of your personal data with these services beyond what is necessary to load the resources.

6. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing cookies. Check your browser's help documentation for instructions.
  • Logging Out: When you log out of ScoutLoot, your session data is cleared.
  • Clear Local Storage: You can clear local storage through your browser's developer tools or by clearing all site data.

Note: Disabling cookies may prevent you from logging in to ScoutLoot, as we use local storage to maintain your session.

7. Do Not Track

ScoutLoot does not track users across websites. We respect Do Not Track (DNT) browser signals, though since we don't track users anyway, your experience will be the same regardless of your DNT setting.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. More Information

For more details about how we handle your data, please read our Privacy Policy.
