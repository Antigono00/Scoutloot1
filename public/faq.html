Getting Started
ScoutLoot is a deal alert service for LEGO collectors. We monitor eBay listings across 5 EU marketplaces and compare prices to BrickLink market data. When prices drop below your target, we send instant Telegram alerts. More marketplaces coming soon!
  1. Create a free account on ScoutLoot
  2. Connect your Telegram account
  3. Add LEGO sets to your watchlist with your target prices
  4. Wait for alerts to arrive when deals match your criteria!

Telegram
Telegram allows us to send you instant, reliable notifications directly to your phone. Unlike email, Telegram messages arrive immediately and don't get lost in spam folders. It's free, fast, and secure.
  1. Open Telegram and search for @ScoutLootBot
  2. Send the /start command
  3. The bot will give you a code to enter in your ScoutLoot settings
  4. That's it! You'll start receiving alerts.
Currently, we only support Telegram notifications. We chose Telegram because it's free, has excellent bot support, and delivers messages instantly. Email notifications may be added in the future for paid subscribers.
