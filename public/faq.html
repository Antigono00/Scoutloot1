Getting Started
ScoutLoot is a deal alert service for LEGO collectors. We monitor eBay listings across USA, Canada, UK, and EU marketplaces and find the best deals. When prices drop below your target, we send instant Telegram or browser push alerts. More marketplaces coming soon!
  1. Create a free account on ScoutLoot
  2. Connect your Telegram account or enable browser push notifications
  3. Add LEGO sets to your watchlist with your target prices
  4. Wait for alerts to arrive when deals match your criteria!
Yes! We offer a free tier that lets you track up to 3 LEGO sets. For more advanced features like instant Telegram alerts, more watches, and priority scanning, check out our Collector and Dealer plans.
How It Works
We scan eBay marketplaces in your region — USA, Canada, UK, or Europe (Germany, France, Spain, Italy). More marketplaces (BrickOwl, Amazon, retailers) coming soon!
Yes! All prices shown include estimated shipping to your country. For cross-border purchases (like UK to EU or EU to UK), we also calculate and show estimated import charges. The total you see is the total you pay.
Our system scans marketplaces every 15 minutes during active hours (7am-11pm your time). When a deal matching your criteria appears, you'll typically receive a Telegram or push notification within minutes. For paid subscribers, alerts are sent instantly without batching delays.
To prevent spam, we only alert you once per day for each LEGO set—unless a better deal appears. If a cheaper listing is found after your first alert, you'll get a new notification immediately. This ensures you always know about the best deal without being overwhelmed.
Notifications
We offer two notification channels:
  • Telegram: Most reliable, instant delivery to your phone
  • Browser Push: Works on desktop and mobile browsers, even when the site is closed
You can use both at the same time for maximum coverage!
  1. Open Telegram and search for @BrickAlpha_bot
  2. Send the /start command
  3. The bot will automatically link to your ScoutLoot account
  4. That's it! You'll start receiving alerts.
Currently, we support Telegram and browser push notifications. We chose Telegram because it's free, has excellent bot support, and delivers messages instantly. Email notifications may be added in the future for paid subscribers.

Troubleshooting
Common reasons:
  • Your target price may be too low for current market prices
  • Telegram may not be connected properly—try reconnecting
  • Browser push notifications may be blocked—check your browser settings
  • You may have reached your daily alert limit
  • The set may be rare with few listings
Check your watch settings and try increasing your target price slightly.
Hot deals can sell within minutes! While we scan frequently and alert quickly, popular sets at low prices get snapped up fast. We recommend enabling both Telegram and push notifications on your phone so you see alerts immediately.
